Private banking service - case study

A client recently sold their business and was due to receive a large deposit into their high street bank account.

The large transactions caused their existing bank to freeze their account on multiple occasions, creating numerous delays and frustrations to the client. As a result, the client approached their Partner to understand whether there any alternative options available to them. The client was introduced to one of our private banks who were able to take a more personalised approach, understanding their particular needs, with the client now receiving an excellent and professional service tailored to their new level of wealth and banking requirements.
